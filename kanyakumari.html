FAMOUS FOOD IN KANYAKUMARI


Fish consumption offers a wealth of health benefits due to its rich nutrient profile, particularly omega-3 fatty acids.
These fatty acids are linked to improved heart health, brain function, and reduced risk of chronic diseases.
Fish also provides high-quality protein, essential vitamins (like D and B2), and minerals (like iodine, zinc, and iron).

Here's a more detailed look at the benefits:

Heart Health:
Omega-3s in fish can lower blood pressure, reduce triglyceride levels, and slow the buildup of plaque in arteries, lowering the risk of heart disease and stroke.
