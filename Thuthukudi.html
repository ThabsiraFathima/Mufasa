Thuthukudi


Famous food in thuthukudi:


Thoothukudi macaroons,

a specialty of the port city in Tamil Nadu, are unique because they use cashews instead of almonds, the traditional ingredient.

This cashew-based recipe was adapted from the European macaroon, introduced to the region by Portuguese sailors.

The macaroons are known for their crispy exterior and chewy interior, a result of the unique preparation method involving cashew powder, egg whites, and sugar.
