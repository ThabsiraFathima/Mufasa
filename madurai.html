FAMOUS FOOD IN Madurai



Jigarthanda,

a popular beverage in Madurai, is known for its unique blend of ingredients and its cooling effect, making it a favorite during the hot summer months.

The name, translating to "cool heart" in Hindi, reflects its purpose of providing a refreshing and soothing experience.

Here are some interesting facts about Jigarthanda: Cooling Properties:

Jigarthanda is believed to have cooling properties due to its ingredients, particularly almond pisin (gum), which is traditionally used as a remedy for heat-related issues.

Unique Ingredients:
It features a combination of milk, almond pisin, basundi, nannari syrup, and ice cream or khoya, creating a distinct taste and texture.
